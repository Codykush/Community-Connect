Our Mission

Preserving traditional crafts by connecting artisans with the world

The ArtisanConnect Story

Founded in 2023, ArtisanConnect began as a small initiative to help local potters in Rajasthan find customers beyond their villages. What started as a simple directory has grown into a movement supporting hundreds of artisans across India.

We believe every handmade piece tells a story - of tradition, skill, and the artisan's dedication. Our platform brings these stories to you, creating meaningful connections between makers and appreciators.

Our Core Values

Artisan Empowerment

We provide tools and platforms for artisans to reach global markets while maintaining fair pricing and credit for their work.

Cultural Preservation

By creating demand for traditional crafts, we help ensure these skills are passed to future generations.

Community Building

We foster connections between artisans for collaboration and between makers and buyers for mutual appreciation.
